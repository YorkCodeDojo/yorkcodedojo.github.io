Tank Battle

Following on with our AI bot theme, this month we will be trying to write bots to destroy each other's tanks!

The session usually begins with a description of the problem, followed by grouping into pairs to work on it, and brief presentation of solutions at the end. This means you need to find somebody to work with on the day. All languages are OK to use - as long as you can find somebody to pair with! Bring a laptop, with plenty of charge :)

Isotoma have kindly offered to sponsor us, and they are conveniently located in the center of York (Buzz the door to get in)

Doors open from 18:00, pizza at 18:15, coding from 18:45, pub around 21:00.
