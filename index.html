York CODE Dojo

Who we are

We're a group based in the city of York who are dedicated to the practical side of programming and learning by doing.

Want to have some fun coding in a relaxed and friendly environment? Interested in self-improvement through deliberate practice? Want to see what you can learn from fellow programmers, and teach them what you know? Then York Code Dojo is for you!

Most events involve working on a coding problem in pairs or small groups. It's an opportunity to try new coding languages and practices, meet people, and have fun!

All languages and levels of experience welcome.
